- Michael Peterson

Mike was born during the spring of 1998 in Utah, but spent most of his youth in Iowa. There, during his middle school and high school years, he further developed his skills as a maker, and began pursuing his interests in computer science. He moved to California as a young adult, and currently works for Wonderful Pistachios and Almonds as a web developer. Mike has a great sense of humor, loves to go on adventures, and refuses to think inside the box. He has experience as an entrepreneur that he plans to utilize in future business ventures, and he can tell you anything and everything you need to know about laser cutting. He is kind-hearted and caring, and looks forward to many years of adventuring with Kat.

- Our Engagement

Kat and Mike met at a social event for young adults in Bakersfield, shortly after Mike relocated to California. They remained friends for almost three years before dating, and have been nearly inseparable since. Mike proposed to Kat in January of 2021 at the Cal Poly Architecture Graveyard in San Luis Obispo. The couple is currently working on starting a laser-cutting business together, and they dream of tackling many more ambitions in the years to come. They happily anticipate the adventures and challenges that await them.
